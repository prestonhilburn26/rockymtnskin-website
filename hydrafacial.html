HydraFacial in Salt Lake City, UT

Clean, clear, and deeply hydrated in one session. The HydraFacial is one of the most effective single treatments for Utah's dry climate — immediate results, zero downtime.

The HydraFacial: Built for Utah Skin

Salt Lake City's dry desert climate is tough on skin. Between the low humidity, high altitude UV exposure, and indoor heating that sucks every last drop of moisture out of the air — Utah skin needs serious, consistent hydration.

The HydraFacial uses patented Vortex-Fusion technology to perform four simultaneous functions: deep cleansing, exfoliation, painless extraction, and intensive serum infusion — all in a single session.

The result? Visibly clearer, smoother, more hydrated skin — with zero downtime. Most clients look and feel better immediately after leaving the studio.

The Process

What's in a HydraFacial Session?

Step 1

Cleanse + Peel

A gentle resurfacing solution loosens dead skin cells and opens pores. Unlike traditional peels, there's no visible flaking or downtime.

Step 2

Extract + Hydrate

The Vortex tip creates a painless suction that lifts out impurities while simultaneously delivering hydrating serum into the now-clean pore. The signature step.

Step 3

Fuse + Protect

Antioxidants, peptides, and hyaluronic acid are infused into the skin to maximize results and leave a visibly dewy, plump finish.

Optional Boosters

We can add targeted booster serums for acne, brightening, aging, or sensitivity — customized to what your skin needs most at the time of your treatment.

Ideal Candidates

Who Should Get a HydraFacial?

HydraFacial is suitable for virtually all skin types and tones. It's a great option for:

  • First-time facial clients looking for a safe, gentle introduction to professional skincare
  • Clients who want real results without any downtime
  • Anyone dealing with dryness, dullness, or congestion
  • Clients with sensitive skin who can't tolerate traditional peels
  • Pre-event treatments — results are immediate and last up to a week
  • Clients with oily or combination skin prone to congestion

Monthly HydraFacial treatments produce the best long-term results. Clients with congestion or active concerns may benefit from every 3–4 weeks initially.

What It Treats

  • ✦Congested pores and blackheads
  • ✦Dryness and dehydration
  • ✦Fine lines and dullness
  • ✦Uneven skin tone and early sun damage
  • ✦Oily or combination skin
  • ✦Acne-prone and sensitive skin
