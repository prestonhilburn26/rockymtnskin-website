Luxury Skincare, At Your Door

House Calls in the Greater Salt Lake Area

Turn any touch-based service into a house call. Same expert care, same results-driven approach — in the comfort of your own home.

Rocky Mountain Skin Comes to You

Not everyone can make it to the studio — and you shouldn't have to miss out on expert skincare because of it. Whether you're a busy parent, recovering from a procedure, hosting a group event, or simply prefer the privacy and comfort of home, house calls bring the full Rocky Mountain Skin experience to your door.

Jake Howard (he/they) is a licensed Master Esthetician who brings professional-grade products, tools, and clinical expertise directly to you. Same thorough skin assessment. Same custom-built treatment. Just in your space, on your terms.

House calls are available throughout the greater Salt Lake area, with a small travel fee based on distance.

Available Services

What You Can Book as a House Call

Any touch-based service travels. If it doesn't require specialized clinic equipment, it comes to you.

  • ✦Custom Facials — full consultation, treatment, and debrief
  • ✦Dermaplaning
  • ✦Microneedling
  • ✦Brow shaping, tinting, and lamination
  • ✦Lash lift and tint
  • ✦Body treatments
  • ✦Multi-service combos — build your own session

Services requiring specialized equipment — including HydraFacial, laser hair reduction, and IPL — are available at our clinic location only.

How It Works

Booking a House Call

1. Choose Your Service

Browse the services above and pick what your skin needs — or combine services for a full session. House calls have a $150 service minimum (before travel fee) to make the trip worthwhile for everyone.

2. Book Online + Add Your Zone

Book your service through our normal online booking. Then add the House Call travel fee for your zone (Zone 1, 2, or 3) as an add-on. Not sure which zone you're in? Just book the service and we'll sort it out.

3. Share Your Setup Details

After booking, you'll get a confirmation with a quick checklist: a comfortable chair or table area, good lighting, and access to a sink nearby. Nothing fancy — I bring everything else.

4. Jake Comes to You

Same thorough consultation, same custom-built treatment, same results. The only difference is you're already home when it's over — no driving, no parking, no rushing back.
