The Brow Bar

Your brows and lashes frame your entire face. When they're shaped and defined well, everything else falls into place. When they're not — nothing else quite works.

The Brow Bar isn't an afterthought at Rocky Mountain Skin. Every shaping, lamination, or lift is approached with the same precision and attention to your individual features that we bring to our skincare treatments.

Whether you're looking for a clean, low-maintenance shape or a bold, sculpted look — we'll design something that actually fits your face, your lifestyle, and your vibe.

Our Services

The Brow Bar Menu

Brow Shaping

Precision wax and/or thread shaping designed around your natural brow bone, face shape, and personal aesthetic. Whether you prefer a soft arch or a straight, bold look — we shape to your features, not a trend.

Duration: 20–30 min  |  Results last 3–5 weeks

Brow Lamination

Brow lamination lifts and sets your brow hairs upward and into place, creating a fuller, more defined shape without microblading or tattooing. Great for unruly, sparse, or downward-growing brows that won't cooperate on their own.

Duration: 45–60 min  |  Results last 6–8 weeks

Brow Tint

Semi-permanent tint adds depth, dimension, and definition to your brows — making sparse areas look fuller and reducing the need for daily brow makeup. Can be done alone or paired with shaping or lamination.

Duration: 15–20 min  |  Results last 3–5 weeks

Lash Lift

A lash lift curls your natural lashes from the root — giving them a dramatic, wide-eyed lift that mascara just can't replicate. Zero extensions, zero maintenance. Just your own lashes, beautifully lifted.

Duration: 45–60 min  |  Results last 6–8 weeks

Lash Tint

Darkens your natural lashes so they look fuller and more defined — even without mascara. Often paired with a lash lift for a complete low-maintenance lash look.

Duration: 20–30 min  |  Results last 3–5 weeks

Brow Lamination + Tint Combo

Our most popular brow service. Lamination sets your hairs in place while tint adds depth and definition. The result is a full, sculpted brow that looks effortlessly done — and lasts for weeks.

Duration: 60–75 min  |  Results last 6–8 weeks

What to Expect

Before & After Your Appointment

Before Brow Lamination or Lash Lift

Arrive with clean, product-free brows and lashes. Remove eye makeup and contacts before your appointment. Avoid retinoids or exfoliating acids around the eye area for 48 hours prior. Let us know if you've had any recent eye procedures or use prescription eye drops.

After Brow Lamination or Lash Lift

For the first 24–48 hours: keep brows and lashes dry, avoid steam, saunas, and swimming, and don't apply makeup or products to the treated area. After that, you're good to go. For lash lifts, use a lash serum to maintain strength and fullness between appointments.

Brow Shaping Aftercare

You may experience some minor redness immediately after waxing — this typically fades within an hour. Avoid touching the area and skip heavy makeup right after. Use SPF on the brow area to prevent post-wax hyperpigmentation, especially in Utah's intense sun.
