Acne & Accutane

PCA Skin Routine for Accutane Clients

By Jake Howard, Licensed Master Esthetician  •  November 5, 2025

Accutane changes how your skin functions—especially in a place like Salt Lake City, where high altitude, low humidity, and intense UV exposure already challenge the skin barrier. PCA Skin offers clinical-grade formulations that are gentle, hydrating, and ideal for compromised, sensitive, or severely dry skin.

This guide breaks down a minimal, effective PCA routine specifically for Accutane users living in Utah’s high desert climate.

Why PCA Skin Is Ideal for Accutane Clients

Accutane thins the skin, reduces oil production, increases dryness, and heightens sensitivity. That makes aggressive actives and harsh exfoliants completely off-limits. PCA formulas are:

  • Fragrance-free
  • Barrier-friendly
  • Dermatologist-trusted
  • Rich in ceramides, peptides, and hydration
  • Designed for compromised or sensitized skin

For Utah clients—who already struggle with dryness, pollution, and tight air—PCA’s soothing, non-irritating formulations are an ideal pairing with isotretinoin.

The Rules of Skincare on Accutane

Before we break down the routine, here’s what absolutely needs to be paused:

Ingredients to avoid:

  • Retinol, retinal, tretinoin
  • Benzoyl peroxide
  • Salicylic acid
  • Glycolic or lactic acids
  • Vitamin C (most forms)
  • Scrubs, devices, or aggressive exfoliation

What you need instead:

  • Gentle cleansing
  • Deep hydration
  • Barrier repair
  • Occlusive moisture
  • Strict sun protection

The routine below is built around exactly that.

The PCA Accutane Routine (Minimal Version)

Designed to be affordable, simple, and highly effective.

AM Routine

1. Cleanser – PCA Creamy Cleanser

A gentle, non-foaming cleanser that removes oil and debris without stripping an already-compromised barrier. Ideal for Utah’s dry climate and Accutane’s dryness-multiplying effect.

Why it works for Accutane:

  • Doesn’t sting
  • Doesn’t foam
  • Doesn’t cause tightness after rinsing

2. Hydration Serum (Optional but Highly Recommended) – PCA Hyaluronic Acid Boosting Serum

If a client can only add one “upgrade” product during Accutane, this is it.

Why this serum matters:

  • Multi-weight HA for deep + surface hydration
  • Sodium PCA + ceramides for barrier strengthening
  • Immediate relief from flaking and tightness

Clients feel the difference quickly, which makes it an easy retail recommendation.

3. Moisturizer – PCA ReBalance

A lightweight, calming moisturizer that works for acne-prone clients while supporting barrier health.

Key benefits:

  • Ceramides for repair
  • Antioxidants to calm inflammation
  • Non-greasy finish (acne clients appreciate this)

It pairs well with SPF and avoids the heaviness that makes many acne clients nervous.

4. Sun Protection – PCA Sheer Tint SPF 45 or another PCA mineral SPF

Mineral sunscreen is the safest option on Accutane. At Utah’s elevation, UV exposure is stronger and more damaging—especially when the skin is thin and dry.

Why PCA SPF:

  • Elegant textures
  • Mineral protection
  • Non-irritating
  • Reliable altitude-friendly coverage

Daily SPF is non-negotiable during Accutane.

PM Routine

1. Cleanser – PCA Creamy Cleanser

Repeat from the morning routine.

2. Serum (Optional) – PCA Hyaluronic Acid Boosting Serum

Using it twice daily is ideal for severe dryness, winter weather, or older clients on Accutane.

3. Barrier Repair Moisturizer – PCA Silkcoat Balm

This is the Accutane moisturizer.

Why Silkcoat is essential:

  • Rich, balm-like occlusive texture
  • Extreme hydration
  • Protects fragile skin from micro-cracking
  • Ideal for SLC winters, inversion season, and dry air

This is the hero nighttime product for most Accutane clients.

4. Lip Care – PCA Hyaluronic Acid Lip Booster or PCA Overnight Lip Mask

Accutane causes nearly universal lip dryness. These PCA options work well for:

  • Flaking
  • Corner cracking
  • Nostrils
  • Cuticles
  • Windburn from Utah outdoor activities

Clients will use these constantly—great for retail and real comfort.

Weekly Treatment

PCA Oat Milk Mask

A soothing, hydrating mask ideal for Accutane flare-ups, winter dryness, or sensitized skin.

Why it works:

  • Oat milk + oat extract for anti-redness support
  • Aloe + cucumber for cooling and calming
  • Rich humectants for immediate comfort

You can retail it or use it in the treatment room for Accutane-safe facials.
