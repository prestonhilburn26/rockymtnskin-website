The Person Behind the Studio

About Jake Howard

Licensed Master Esthetician. Founder of Rocky Mountain Skin. He/they. Based in South Salt Lake, UT.

Hi. I'm Jake.

I'm a licensed Master Esthetician and the founder of Rocky Mountain Skin — a skincare studio in South Salt Lake, UT built around the belief that good skin care should be accessible, personalized, and free of judgment.

I got into esthetics because I've lived through skin struggles myself. Acne, hormonal changes, the particular challenge of Utah's altitude and desert climate — I know what it's like to feel like nothing is working. That personal experience shapes how I approach every client: we start with your actual skin, not a template, and we build from there.

I hold a Master Esthetician license in Utah and have completed advanced clinical training in treatments including microneedling, laser technology, and chemical exfoliation. I stay current on the science — because the skincare industry changes fast, and I want my clients to have access to treatments that actually work.

Outside the treatment room, I'm queer, I'm curious, and I care deeply about building a space where people feel welcome — especially people who've felt like they didn't belong in traditional spa settings. That includes trans clients, non-binary clients, clients navigating body changes from HRT, and anyone who's felt like "wellness" wasn't made for them.

It was made for you here.

The Studio

About Rocky Mountain Skin

Rocky Mountain Skin opened in South Salt Lake, UT with one goal: to offer clinical-level skincare in a space that feels nothing like a clinical environment.

This is a one-person studio. That's intentional. When you book with Rocky Mountain Skin, you always work with Jake — not a rotation of providers, not a franchise menu, not a 45-minute assembly line facial. Just focused, personalized care every single time.

The studio is located at 2253 S State St, Unit 4 in South Salt Lake — easy to get to from anywhere in the Salt Lake Valley, with parking on-site.

We're proud to serve clients from South Salt Lake, Salt Lake City, Murray, Millcreek, Holladay, West Valley, and across the greater Salt Lake area.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Queer-Affirming

This is a space where queer, trans, and non-binary clients are genuinely welcomed — not just tolerated. All pronouns honored. Gender-affirming care incorporated where it matters.

Results-Driven

We don't believe in treatments for the sake of selling treatments. Everything we recommend is based on what your skin actually needs — and honest about what to expect.

Education First

You'll leave every appointment understanding what we did and why. Skincare literacy is part of the service — because great skin happens between appointments, too.
