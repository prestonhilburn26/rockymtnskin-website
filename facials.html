Facials in Salt Lake City, UT

Every facial here is built around therapeutic touch, extended massage, and expert technique. Choose your level: a quick reset, a full basecamp session, or the summit experience.

Skincare That Feels as Good as It Works

At Rocky Mountain Skin, facials aren't rushed routines. They're therapeutic experiences built around what your skin actually needs — with extended massage, targeted manual techniques, and real expertise guiding every step.

Jake Howard is a licensed Master Esthetician with advanced clinical training, specializing in the unique challenges of Utah skin. High altitude, intense UV, and desert dry air demand more than a standard facial. Every treatment here accounts for that.

Whether you need a quick 35-minute refresh between meetings or a full two-hour immersion, the approach is the same: listen to your skin, choose the right techniques, and make the experience worth your time.

What We Address

Concerns We Treat

  • ✦Acne, breakouts, and congested pores
  • ✦Dryness, dehydration, and compromised barrier
  • ✦Uneven skin tone and hyperpigmentation
  • ✦Fine lines and early signs of aging
  • ✦Sensitivity, redness, and reactive skin
  • ✦Dull, rough, or uneven texture
  • ✦Post-procedure and post-surgical barrier repair
  • ✦Hormonally shifting skin (HRT, puberty, pregnancy, menopause)
  • ✦Puffiness, poor circulation, and lymphatic congestion
