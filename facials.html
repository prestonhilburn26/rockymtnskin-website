Custom Facial in Salt Lake City, UT

Your skin isn't like anyone else's — so why would your facial be? Every treatment is built from the ground up around what your skin actually needs that day.

Skincare Built Entirely Around You

At Rocky Mountain Skin, every Custom Facial is designed from the ground up around what your skin actually needs. Not a menu-based service. Not a timed template. Not a one-size-fits-all treatment.

Just real, targeted skincare — with a licensed esthetician who looks at your skin before choosing a single product.

Jake Howard (he/they) is a licensed Master Esthetician with advanced clinical training, specializing in Utah skin. Our high altitude, intense UV, and desert dry air create unique challenges — and every treatment here accounts for that.

What We Address

What the Custom Facial Treats

  • ✦Acne, breakouts, and congested pores
  • ✦Dryness, dehydration, and a compromised barrier
  • ✦Uneven skin tone and hyperpigmentation
  • ✦Fine lines and early signs of aging
  • ✦Sensitivity, redness, and reactive skin
  • ✦Dull, rough, or uneven texture
  • ✦Post-Accutane and post-surgical barrier repair
  • ✦Hormonally shifting skin (HRT, puberty, pregnancy, menopause)

Step by Step

What Happens During Your Custom Facial

Consultation + Skin Assessment

We start by talking through your skin history, current routine, and goals. Then I examine your skin under magnification — mapping what's actually happening before I choose a single product or technique.

Double Cleanse

Tailored to your skin type. The first pass removes makeup, sunscreen, and environmental buildup. The second actually cleans your skin. No stripping, no harsh foam — just a clean canvas.

Exfoliation

Enzyme exfoliation, a light acid, or gentle manual exfoliation — whichever your skin needs. This lifts dead cells, unclogs pores, and primes your skin for active ingredients.

Extractions (When Appropriate)

For congested or acne-prone skin, professional manual extractions clear pores carefully and hygienically — without trauma or scarring.

Treatment Mask

A customized mask based on your primary concern: hydration, brightening, calming, or clarifying. While it works, most clients enjoy a relaxing massage for the face, neck, and shoulders.

Finishing Products

Serums, moisturizer, and SPF are layered on to lock in results and protect your skin on the way out.

The Debrief

Before you leave, we talk through what I found, what your skin responded to, and what to do at home to maintain your results. No hard sell — just real advice.
