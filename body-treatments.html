Body Rituals

Body Treatments in Salt Lake City, UT

Utah-inspired body rituals built around exfoliation, targeted bodywork, and recovery. From a quick 30-minute polish to a full 90-minute summit ritual — every service extends beyond the face.

Your Skin Doesn't Stop at Your Jawline

Body treatments at Rocky Mountain Skin are built on the same philosophy as our facials: therapeutic touch, manual technique, and real attention to what your skin needs. These aren't spa fillers — they're focused bodywork experiences designed for the dry air, active lifestyle, and altitude of living in Utah.

Back facials, full-body exfoliation, booty treatments, CBD recovery — whatever you're here for, the approach is the same: professional, thorough, and worth your time.
