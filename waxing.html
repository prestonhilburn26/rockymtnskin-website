Waxing That Doesn't Have to Be Awkward

Whether it's a quick brow cleanup or a full Manzilian, the experience here is the same: professional, efficient, and comfortable. Jake is a licensed Master Esthetician who's done thousands of wax services — there's nothing you need to feel self-conscious about.

Rocky Mountain Skin uses high-quality hard wax and soft wax, selected based on the area being treated and your skin sensitivity. Hard wax grips hair without sticking to skin, making it ideal for sensitive and intimate areas. Soft wax is used for larger, flatter areas where speed and efficiency matter.

Before Your Wax

Prep Tips

  • ✦Hair should be at least ¼ inch long (about 2–3 weeks of growth)
  • ✦Exfoliate gently 1–2 days before (not the day of)
  • ✦Avoid retinoids and exfoliating acids for 3–5 days before
  • ✦Skip caffeine right before if you're sensitive to pain
  • ✦Come with clean, product-free skin in the wax area

After Your Wax

Aftercare

  • ✦Avoid heat — no saunas, hot tubs, or intense workouts for 24 hours
  • ✦Skip tight clothing on freshly waxed areas
  • ✦Don't touch or pick at the area
  • ✦Exfoliate gently starting 3 days after to prevent ingrowns
  • ✦Apply SPF to any waxed areas exposed to sun
